About HMG SermonScribe v3

HMG SermonScribe v3 is an enterprise-grade, offline-first, zero-cost sermon transcription and church intelligence platform. It empowers pastors, Bible study leaders, and church members to capture, organize, and share sermon content without relying on expensive AI APIs or cloud subscriptions.

Built by Adewale Samson Adeagbo under HMG Concepts (His Marvellous Grace), this FaithTech product serves the Nigerian church and believers worldwide.

Philosophy

"Real problems. Real solutions. Built with what is available, not what is expensive."

We use the Web Speech API, IndexedDB, BroadcastChannel, Service Workers, and native browser capabilities to deliver a fully installable, offline-capable Progressive Web App (PWA).
