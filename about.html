Why This Exists

Across Nigerian churches, members struggle to take accurate sermon notes while the pastor is preaching. Phones are distracting, handwriting is too slow, and many believers miss the deeper points because they are busy trying to capture surface-level quotes.

HMG SermonScribe v2 was built to solve this with real-time, browser-based speech-to-text transcription. No typing required. The device listens as the pastor speaks, and the text appears instantly. Members can focus on worship and understanding, while the app captures the message.

Most importantly, it is built under the same conviction that shapes everything HMG does: Real problems. Real solutions. Built with what is available, not what is expensive.

Cost Discipline

This platform deliberately avoids paid AI APIs, monthly subscriptions, or server costs that make technology inaccessible to small churches. It uses the Web Speech API built into modern browsers and stores everything in your device using IndexedDB.
